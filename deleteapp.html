How to Delete Your Account

Last Updated: 7/30/2024

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your LooxMaxApp account, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the LooxMaxApp on your device.
  2. In the top right corner of the screen, click on your profile picture.
  3. From the menu that appears, select Delete Account.
  4. A pop-up will appear asking you to confirm your decision. Click on Delete Account in the pop-up to finalize the deletion.

After completing these steps, your account will be permanently deleted, and all your data will be removed from our system.
